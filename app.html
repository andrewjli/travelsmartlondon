TravelSmart London

TSL is a mobile Android application that aims to provide a better and smarter user experience by placing the focus on the travellers' comfort. Find out more about TSL below and once you've quenched your thirst for knowledge about us, find out yourself by downloading our revolutionary app!

Download now!

No more fragmentation

TSL is one of the few applications that combines all major modes of public transport in London - Underground, Barclay bikes and Busses - in one place. This gives the travellers a single point of access so that they no longer have to face fragmentation and switch between a multitude of different applications! Travellers using TSL will not waste their valuable time finding the right way to go!

Be aware about travelling conditions

TSL provides information about the quality of travel system at any given point in time. Commuters travelling to work every day can use our app to see how crowded their Tube line is so that they can decide to choose alternative routes. In addition, TSL displays information about weather conditions relevant to travellers.

We value your input

As well as displaying public transport information we enable travellers to give feedback about their journeys. What is more, in TSL, users can comment on their journey experience and rate individual Tube lines. By combining feedback from the social networking site Twitter and from withing our app, TSL enables travellers to make smart decisions about their journeys. TSL will be the first application to include traveller sentiment scraped from the popular social networking site Twitter and analysed to reveal current experience of travellers around London (COMING SOON).
